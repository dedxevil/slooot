üí° What is Gratuity?

Gratuity is a sum of money paid by an employer to an employee as a reward for long-standing service. As per the Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972, any organization with 10 or more employees must pay gratuity to eligible employees.

Gratuity is also applicable in cases of:

  • Retirement
  • Superannuation
  • Resignation after 5+ years
  • Sudden death or disability

You're eligible for gratuity if:

  • You have completed 5 or more years in the same organization
  • You retire or resign after this period
  • You face disability due to work
  • You are eligible for superannuation
