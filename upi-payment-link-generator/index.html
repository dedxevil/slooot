UPI Payment Link Generator

How to Use the UPI Payment Link Generator

  • Enter your UPI ID (e.g., yourname@bank) in the input field. This is your unique identifier for receiving UPI payments.
  • Optionally, enter the amount you want to request and a note or message to describe the purpose of the payment.
  • Click the Generate UPI Link button to instantly create a secure UPI payment link.
  • The tool will also generate a QR code for UPI payment that users can scan using any UPI-enabled app like Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, or BHIM.
  • Use the Copy Link button to copy the UPI link to your clipboard for easy sharing via WhatsApp, SMS, or email.
  • Click Download QR Code to save the QR image for printing or embedding on websites, invoices, or receipts.
  • Ensure your UPI ID is valid and active to receive payments without issues.

This free UPI Payment Link Generator is ideal for freelancers, small businesses, NGOs, and anyone who wants to collect payments online without sharing bank account details. It supports all major UPI apps and is optimized for fast, secure, and contactless transactions. Whether you're collecting donations, selling products, or requesting service payments, this tool simplifies digital payments with UPI QR codes and UPI deep links.
